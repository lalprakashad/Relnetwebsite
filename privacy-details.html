Summary (Not a substitute for the full policy): We collect the minimum data necessary to operate our website and services, and we do not sell your data.

1) Information We Collect

  • Contact Data: name, email address, phone number, and message content when you contact us.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device information, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: used to operate and improve the Site; you can control cookies via your browser settings.

2) How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide our Services.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site, including analytics.
  • To send service and policy notices (with consent where required).
  • To protect against, investigate, and deter fraudulent or illegal activity.

3) Legal Bases

Where applicable, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases: consent, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests.

4) Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: trusted vendors who assist in operations (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with appropriate safeguards.

5) International Transfers

We may transfer data to countries outside your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable laws.

6) Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law.

7) Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority where applicable.

8) Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain Site features. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

9) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10) Children’s Privacy

Our Site and Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” above reflects the latest revision. Your continued use of the Site after changes become effective signifies acceptance.
